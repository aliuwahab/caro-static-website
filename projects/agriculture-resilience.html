Ongoing programme

Agriculture resilience

The project aims to enhance advocacy efforts at the local level to tackle issues related to women's access to productive resources in agriculture. We work to ensure women can participate fully and benefit from sustainable agricultural development.

Objectives

  • Advocate for women’s access to land, inputs and productive resources in agriculture.
  • Strengthen local-level advocacy and dialogue with traditional and government actors.
  • Support women’s participation in sustainable and resilient agricultural practices.

Our approach

We work with women’s groups, farmers and local leaders to identify barriers and advocate for fair access to resources. Activities include community dialogues, awareness sessions and collaboration with local government and traditional authorities to promote inclusive agricultural development.

Impact

When women have better access to productive resources, families and communities benefit from improved food security and livelihoods. This programme contributes to our broader focus on women empowerment and sustainable development.
