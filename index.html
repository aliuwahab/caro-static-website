Advancing Rural Opportunity

The Centre for Advancing Rural Opportunity (CARO) is a youth-led community development organization dedicated to empowering communities and promoting the well-being of society for a resilient and sustainable future.

What we do

We work across health, WASH, women’s empowerment, governance, and youth development to build resilient communities.

🏥

Rural healthcare

We run rural healthcare outreach and support maternal health and adolescent reproductive health and rights (ARHR).

What we represent

We believe in the inherent strength and potential of every individual and strive to create opportunities that enable them to thrive. Through collaborations and partnerships, education and advocacy, we work to uplift marginalized voices, foster inclusive environments and drive positive social change. Our commitment to empowering communities is rooted in compassion, empathy and a deep understanding of the interconnectedness of our shared humanity. Together, we can create a brighter future where every person has the opportunity to flourish and contribute to a thriving society.
