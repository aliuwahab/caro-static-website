About us

The Centre for Advancing Rural Opportunity (CARO) is a youth-led community development organization dedicated to empowering communities and promoting the well-being of society for a resilient and sustainable community development.

Vision

To see a society of peace and dignity, where everyone, everywhere has equal access to resources and opportunities for development.

Mission

Working in partnership to empower communities, and to promote the well-being of people in society for a resilient and sustainable community development.

Aims and objectives

  • To advocate for access to quality health care and WASH services in rural communities.
  • To give a voice to and promote the rights of women, children and marginalized groups in society.
  • To advocate in collaboration with other development partners for poverty alleviation in poor communities.
  • To empower women and promote youth development for sustainable livelihoods.
  • To promote democracy and foster social accountability for inclusive governance.
  • To promote peace, security and integration for social cohesion in society.

Areas of operation

  1. Health and Nutrition
  2. Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH)
  3. Human Rights Advocacy (Gender inclusion, PWDs, Gender-Based Violence (GBV))
  4. Youth Development and Entrepreneurship
  5. Peace and Security and Social Integration
  6. Social Accountability and Good Governance
  7. Empowerment and Micro enterprising (Women Empowerment)

Core values

Trust

We will ensure and build common trust between us and our constituents as well as with our partners.

Mutual respect

To engender cordiality in the work we do, we shall respect each other's views with the understanding that it is only when we disagree that best alternative decisions will be made to bring about progress. We shall allow all to have a say.

Equity and justice

To work to ensure equal opportunities to all and sundry, irrespective of race, age, gender, social status, economic status, HIV status, religion, ethnicity, disability etc.

Transparency and accountability

We will at all times, without any compromise ensure that transparency and accountability override all our transactions amongst ourselves and the people we work with.

Gender and social inclusion

In all our dealings we will take into consideration the role of both men and women in a way that gives them equal opportunities. We will design and implement programmes and activities that improve the economic and social capacities of women and children with the aim of reaching gender parity.
